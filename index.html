What is Gun Violence ?

Gun Violence is violence committed with firearms, such as handguns, shotguns, rifles etc. More than 600 people die every day from gun violence with some of these gun-related deaths including suicide. Up to 81% of deaths have been caused by firearms. Which marked the highest percentage since the year of 1968
